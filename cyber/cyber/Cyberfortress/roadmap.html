Product Roadmap

CyberFortress is actively developed with quarterly feature releases. Customer feedback drives our product roadmap.

Roadmap Philosophy

We publish our roadmap in quarters (not specific dates) to allow flexibility while maintaining transparency. Features may shift based on customer feedback, security landscape changes, and technical priorities.





2026 Roadmap

Enhanced OT/ICS Support

Detection: Expanded protocol support for industrial control systems (Modbus, DNP3, ICS/SCADA protocols)

Deployment: AWS GovCloud deployment option for government customers

Advanced Threat Hunting & Cloud-Native

Platform: Advanced threat hunting UI with natural language queries and threat intelligence integration

Deployment: Kubernetes-native deployment with Helm charts for cloud-native environments

Post-Quantum Crypto & FedRAMP

Security: Post-quantum cryptography implementation (ML-KEM) for future-proof encryption

Compliance: FedRAMP Moderate authorization for government deployments

Hardware Security Module Integration

Platform: Hardware security module (HSM) integration for enhanced key management and tamper resistance

Preview: Early access to future hardware acceleration product
